About Us

Our muffin journey began with a simple passion in the heart of Bulacan, where my aunt first started baking her delicious, homemade muffins. What began as a small hobby quickly grew into a thriving business, fueled by love, creativity, and the joy of sharing freshly baked goodness. She would post her creations online, allowing customers to see the products and place their orders by the number of boxes. Each muffin is baked with care using her own recipes, ensuring every bite tastes like home. Today, that same tradition continues here — bringing the comforting flavor of our family’s kitchen to yours.
